/my process

Do you have a strong web presence?

What will customers experience when they visit your website on their phones? In today's digital landscape, an accessible and responsive website is essential. My passion lies in helping small businesses not only establish but elevate their online presence. Whether that be creating a new website for you from the ground up or refreshing an existing one, this is the place to be. Here is how I deliver success:

1. Consultation

It all starts with a conversation to discuss your vision for your website.

2. Proposal

Based on our consultation, I will draft a thorough proposal outlining a mockup, project costs, timeline, and details.

3. Creation

We begin the exciting process of building your website! Throughout the process, you will receive consistent updates regarding the state of your project.

4. Launch & Support

Once the site is complete and you are satisfied, we will go live and the final project will be launched. Post-production, I can provide ongoing support, taking care of any maintenance needs.
