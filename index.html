/about me

— from business banking to coding

Most of my career was spent working in the financial industry, where I've provided support to Business & Commercial Account Managers with operational risk policies, procedures, and technical support for credit application systems. It has been the highlight of my career being able to use my problem-solving and analytical skills to investigate new and diverse situations every day.

However, I've always had a profound interest in technology. Combining this with my knack for problem-solving has led to my true passion: coding. I now create things that live on the web.

When I'm not coding, I love weight-lifting, discovering new cafes and reading books.
